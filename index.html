About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Electronic Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong(CUHK), supervised by Hon Ki Tsang. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in June 2020 in Opto-Electronic Information Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST).
My current research interests focus on Silicon Photonics, integrated spectrometer and optical phased array.
